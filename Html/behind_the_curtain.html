2. Desgin thinking

  • My design thinking was probably first trying to find a suitable colour combination, then because I prefer the Laker colours I put purple and yellow together as colours that go with each other. Then I followed the more traditional website design idea of placing the header at the top of the site with the navigation bar below it. The body of my page was then divided into sections using horizontal bars, each section focusing on a specific piece of content, with the subheadings in each section in a slightly larger font and in dark purple, and the body in mauve, with a box framing the body, and mauve used as the background colour.
