Read-aloud Function

Accessible Voice Read Aloud Function Statement

In order to make information easily accessible to all users, I have developed an accessible voice read aloud feature on this website specifically with the aim of providing a more user-friendly access experience for users who are visually impaired, dyslexic, or those who prefer auditory learning. I have fixed the bottom right corner of the webpage with 'fixed' so that the accessibility button will always appear in the webpage, and the viewer can click on the “read” button to have the webpage read aloud, and click on the '-'' and '+' buttons to have the webpage read aloud. The viewer can click on the 'read' button to have the web page read aloud, and click on '-' and '+'to increase or slow down the speed of speech, and click on 'stop'to stop the reading function.
